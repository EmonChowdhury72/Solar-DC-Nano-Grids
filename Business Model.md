## ðŸ’° Revenue & Sustainability Approach
To ensure financial viability and ease of adoption, the project explores multiple sustainable approaches:

1.  **Direct System Sales:** Sales directly to established vendors and small businesses.
2.  **Strategic Partnerships:** Collaboration with NGOs, city councils, and market associations for bulk deployment.
3.  **Microfinance Models:** Ownership models backed by microfinance institutions to ease upfront payment.
4.  **Flexible Payments:** Implementation of Pay-as-you-go (PAYG) or installment-based payment options.

*These approaches effectively reduce adoption barriers while ensuring the long-term viability of the system.*
