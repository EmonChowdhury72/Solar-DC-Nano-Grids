## Mission

To provide **reliable, affordable, and clean electricity** for small vendors and micro-entrepreneurs in Bangladesh by reducing dependence on unreliable grid power and costly diesel generators through modular Solar-DC nano-grid systems.

---

## Vision

To develop a **scalable, modular, and data-driven Solar-DC Nano-Grid model** that can be locally assembled, easily maintained, and replicated across urban and peri-urban markets in Bangladesh and South Asia.

---

## General Project Information

SolarSpark Bangladesh is building a **DC-only solar energy solution** designed for small businesses affected by frequent load-shedding.  
The system integrates:

- Solar power generation  
- MPPT-based battery charging  
- Lithium-ion energy storage  
- Regulated DC output  
- Real-time monitoring and data collection  

The goal is to ensure **stable power, lower energy costs, longer operating hours, and reduced environmental impact** for small vendors.
